We are the first generation whose daily tools include systems that learn faster
than we do.

Most responses to AI fall into one of two extremes:

1. **Panic** — the fear that everything meaningful will be automated away.
2. **Delegation** — the temptation to let the machine think so we do not have to.

Homo Adapticus looks for a third path:

Use AI to *amplify* attention, not replace it.

Use models to run more experiments, not to abdicate responsibility.
